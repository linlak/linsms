What are LinSMS key features?

Whether you want to send one message or millions, LinSMS is a feature rich platiform that allows you to manage everything related to sending SMS messages online.
Create or upload contact lists, use merge features for bulk messaging (Repeated numbers are filtered). Our fully featured business ready, web sms platform is packed with plenty of features to help you create even the most complex SMS campaigns.

Contact list & Custom fields

Easily import contacts into pre saved lists by aploading CSV, xls or txt files. This allows you to easily segment your data knowing that your contacts are only a click a way.

Merge & Upload send

Merge Send allows you to send messages to your imported contact lists, while Upload Send gives you the flexibility to upload and send directly from a file without saving it.

Reuse Group & Resend

Reuse Group allows you to use the contacts from sent sms details, while Resend allows you to send the same message to the same contact group.
This feature helps you to send SMS anywhere without need to carry notebooks or storage devices every time you need to send an sms.

Export and Download

This option enables our clients to download a copy of their uploaded phonebook group (s). For use with another service or backup before deleting phonebook group (s).

Me2U

Easily share your SMS credit with your friend. This feature enables our users to ask their friends to bail them out in cases of an emmergency when they do not have access to any of the payment platforms we support.
