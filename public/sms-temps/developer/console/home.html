Developer Console

LinSMS developer console is where you will set all of your integration parameters which includes,

  1. Create App
  2. View your apps
  3. Edit your Apps
  4. Get integration credentials
  5. Add your Domain
  6. Verify your domain

To know more on how to integrate into our system, you can read our Tutorials

Definitions

App: This is the top most point of integration which anables you to integrate with LinSMS open Api.

The App is composed of different components which include

ClientId: This is a unique parameter that identifies your App on our system. It can be regenerated if you feel it is licked or someone is trying to impersonate you.

Secret: This acts as your password. This should never be shared or used in insecure environments. The secret can also be regenerate in case it is licked, We recommend updating the secret every after two months.
